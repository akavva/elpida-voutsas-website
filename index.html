About Artist

Born in Athens in 1975.

Education:

  • Attended painting classes at the Painting Workshop of the Municipality of Perama from 2007 to 2011, with teacher Afroditi Sezenia.
  • Studied drawing at the Painting Workshop of K. Pappas & K. Esslin from 2013 to 2014.
  • Admitted to the Athens School of Fine Arts (ΑΣΚΤ) and studied painting at the A' Painting Workshop with professors Angelos Antonopoulos and Manolis Merambelios.
  • During her studies, she learned engraving from professors V. Tsalamata and Akis Pirounidis, ceramics with professor P. Paltoglou, and mosaic with D. Angelidou.
